Fun with AI

Kinetic Communications, a software development company in Birmingham, Alabama, has an online job application on their website that applicants are encouraged to fill out. One of the questions on the application is as follows:

“You are crossing a remote, barren desert. You encounter a man -- dead, naked, and lying face down in the sand -- holding a straw in his hand. What happened?”

Kinetic was expecting a written essay, but, I made a movie instead, writing my own script, and using Runway's text-to-video website to produce the video scenes.
