Programming and design philosophy

I feel strongly about a few things:

  • Take care: whatever you do, do it well.
  • Stepping back: Pause, take a deep breath, and think about what you are trying to do. Don't suck quickly.
  • Knowing your tools: Choose the right tool for the job.
  • Code clarity over cleverness: Ease of understanding wins over fewer lines of code.

...and above all:

  • Less is more
